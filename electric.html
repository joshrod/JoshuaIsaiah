montoya electric

Montoya Electric LLC is a family-owned electric company based in Chicago, Illinois. They perform high quality, efficient work and service residential and commercial areas excellently.

I am currently (April 28th, 2017) tasked with modernizing their company van with a design that is cost-efficient and eye-catching, as well as creating a website that fulfills their business requirements effectively.

Creating the Design

The client wanted something that made people double-take on either the logo or the van. He had a great idea to use a circuit diagram as the main part of the design to give his company an identity.

After converting the words of the logo itself into a diagram, I expanded on an idea that the client mentioned to try as a last resort: to make the Chicago skyline with the diagram's elements.

This was the winner. The client and I continued to work together to figure out the design's placement and colors, which eventually manifested into the van shown above.

We are currently waiting on quotes to make sure the artwork fits the company's budget, and will revise it in case it does not.

Creating the Website

The website is a work in progress at the moment. We are currently in the process of researching competitors, targeting company goals, and gathering content so that the site can optimally benefit the business.

I will then make a mockup of a user-friendly design and report back to the client before going on to code the final product. The mockup will be updated here when it is completed.

epilogue

I am really excited for the opportunity to help a small business move into the future in whatever way I can. I will continue to do my best to help this business succeed and reach their goals so that they may continue providing service in Chicago for years to come.
